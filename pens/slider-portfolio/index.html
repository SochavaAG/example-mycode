Key Features
01 User role distribution

The platform allows splitting the range of activities and opportunities considering the user level. The system provides three intuitive dashboards for students, agents, and partners with descriptions of establishments and requirements, individual profiles, and many more.

02 Personal plans

This feature allows students to tailor the environment to their needs. It generates offers based on personal preferences and data to deliver the most suitable options for each applicant.

03 Search

The platform has an intelligent search module with various filters dividing institutions by categories and niches. It allows users to check all the underlying conditions and requirements, compare them with the applicant's background, and contact recruiters in one click.
