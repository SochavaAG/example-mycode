Our values
  • Behavior
    You make decisions and take responsibility for their implementation. Trying to solve the cause of the problem, not the effect. You understand what needs to be done right now and do it. Your actions and decisions are supported by reliable data and verified facts. Do not wait for instructions on what to do. Your actions do not create excessive bureaucracy.
  • Education
    You never stop learning, gain knowledge in related fields. Try to deeply understand the business, customers, processes. Share information and knowledge proactively.
  • Influence
    Focused on the result, not the process. You are welcome to new important tasks. Colleagues can rely on you. You help colleagues. For you there is no words "this is not my task."
  • Innovation
    Put forward ideas to implement. Offer new best approaches and solutions. Guided by the principles of "make it easier, make more profitable." Implement processes only if they help you do more.
  • Leadership
    Self-motivating Self-improvement Self-disciplined Don't be afraid to make mistakes and learn from them. Take care of your colleagues.
  • Communication
    If you need something - do not be silent, go and ask. Always check if the interlocutor understood correctly. Write letters / reports that can be used further without your help. With understanding and respect, treat the point of view of the interlocutor. You say only what you can say with all participants in the conversation.
