CSS3D Clouds

An experiment on creating volumetric3d-like clouds with CSS3 3D Transforms and a bit of Javascript.

Move the mouse to rotatearound and mouse wheel to zoomin and out. Hit space to generatea new cloud. Works on Firefox(faster if Nightly), Chromeand Safari.
